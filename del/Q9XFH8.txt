CC   -!- FUNCTION: Thiol-disulfide oxidoreductase involved in the redox
CC       regulation of enzymes of both reductive pentose phosphate pathway
CC       (Calvin-Benson cycle) and oxidative pentose phosphate pathway.
CC       Under light or reducing conditions, activates in chloroplast the
CC       glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate dehydrogenase, the phosphoribulokinase
CC       and the fructose-1,6-bisphosphate phosphatase, and inhibits the
CC       glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16263928,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19631646, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19825612}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Plastid, chloroplast stroma
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19259774}.
CC   -!- PTM: Glutathionylation at Cys-126 decreases its ability to be
CC       reduced by ferredoxin-thioredoxin reductase and reduces its
CC       efficiency in activating target chloroplastic enzymes.
