CC   -!- FUNCTION: Actin-depolymerizing protein. Severs actin filaments (F-
CC       actin) and binds to actin monomers (PubMed:19346440,
CC       PubMed:22010035). Contributes to the stochastic dynamic turnover
CC       of actin filaments (PubMed:22010035). Binds monomeric actin (G-
CC       actin) with a marked preference for the ADP-loaded form and
CC       inhibits the rate of nucleotide exchange on G-actin. Involved in
CC       resistance triggered by the effector AvrPphB of Pseudomonas
CC       syringae pv tomato (Pst). May modulate the AvrPphB-RPS5-mediated
CC       defense signal transduction pathway (PubMed:19346440). During
CC       AvrPphB-triggered resistance signaling pathway, involved in the
CC       control of MPK3 and MPK6 activaton, via the coordinated regulation
CC       of actin cytoskeletal dynamics and RPS5 resistance gene
CC       transcription (PubMed:23144618). During innate immune response
CC       triggered by the bacterial elf26 peptide, the inhibition of ADF4
CC       regulates actin dynamics in order to execute key events associated
CC       with pattern-triggered immunity (PTI), such as cell wall
CC       fortification and transcriptional activation of defense gene
CC       markers (PubMed:24464292). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19346440,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22010035, ECO:0000269|PubMed:23144618,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24464292}.
