CC   -!- FUNCTION: Essential protein for photoautotrophism. Confers
CC       resistance to photo-oxidative damages by contributing to the
CC       thermal dissipation of light energy and to lumenal acidification
CC       (increase of pH gradient). Component of the cytochrome b6-f
CC       complex, which mediates electron transfer between photosystem II
CC       (PSII) and photosystem I (PSI), cyclic electron flow around PSI,
CC       and state transitions (By similarity). {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11722777, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12023025,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12970486}.
