CC       xylem cell wall thickening. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11148295,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:12165296, ECO:0000269|PubMed:12805608,

CC       tissues such as xylems and interfascicular regions. Expressed in

CC       localization. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:27387950}.
