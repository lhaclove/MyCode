CC       (PubMed:24151298). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22212719,

CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Plastid, chloroplast outer membrane.
CC       Plastid, chloroplast thylakoid membrane.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in flowers, upper leaves, rosette
CC       and cauline leaves, stem. Not detectable in non-photosynthetic
CC       tissues such as roots and seeds. Expressed in seedlings and adult
CC       plant. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10838072}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Expressed in etiolated seedlings.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11785941}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Circadian-regulation. Not regulated by light.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10838072, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11785941,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:7659751}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: No visible phenotype at the levels of the
CC       whole plant or chloroplast ultrastructure; due to the redundancy
CC       with PORC. Porb and porc double mutants have a seedling-lethal
CC       pale-yellow xantha phenotype at the cotyledon stage, contain only
CC       small amounts of Chla, and possess chloroplasts with mostly
CC       unstacked thylakoid membranes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12848821}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: The presence of TOC33 is required for the import of
CC       PORA into plastids in cotyledons and true leaves.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:15842619}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the short-chain dehydrogenases/reductases
CC       (SDR) family. POR subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
