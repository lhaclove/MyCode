CC   -!- FUNCTION: Actin-depolymerizing protein. Stimulates F-actin
CC       depolymerization. Involved in plant development, cell organ
CC       expansion and flowering by controlling breakdown of thick actin
CC       cables (PubMed:11402164). Severs actin filaments or bundles and
CC       promotes actin cytoskeleton disassembly (PubMed:21570971). Binds
CC       monomeric actin (G-actin) with a marked preference for the ADP-
CC       loaded form and inhibits the rate of nucleotide exchange on G-
CC       actin (PubMed:17538023). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11402164,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17538023, ECO:0000269|PubMed:21570971}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with the 14-3-3-like protein GRF6/AFT1.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26345162}.
