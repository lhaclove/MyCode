CC       development by controlling cell proliferation in root meristem.
CC       May mediate responses to auxin in the root. May act as promoter of
CC       the flowering transition through up-regulation of SOC, FT and LFY.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18203871}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:

CC       ProRule:PRU00251}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Preferentially expressed in roots
CC       (PubMed:7549482). In root meristem, expressed in external cells of
CC       columella, lateral root cap and atrichoblasts. In mature root,
CC       expressed in the central cylinder (PubMed:11855641). Expressed in
CC       leaf vasculature, young floral meristems and nectaries
CC       (PubMed:18203871). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11855641,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18203871, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7549482}.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: During embryo development, expressed in a
CC       punctate pattern from the globular stage to the torpedo stage.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11855641}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: By auxin in root phloem. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18203871}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Retarded root growth, and altered root
CC       meristem size and stem-cell patterning. Late flowering phenotype.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18203871}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: XAANTAL is the Mayan word for "go slower" in
