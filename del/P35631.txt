CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor that promotes early floral meristem
CC       identity in synergy with LEAFY. Is required subsequently for the
CC       transition of an inflorescence meristem into a floral meristem. Is
CC       indispensable for normal development of sepals and petals in
CC       flowers. Regulates positively the B class homeotic proteins
CC       APETALA3 and PISTILLATA with the cooperation of LEAFY and UFO.
CC       Interacts with SEPALLATA3 or AP3/PI heterodimer to form complexes
CC       that could be involved in genes regulation during floral meristem
CC       development. Regulates positively AGAMOUS in cooperation with
CC       LEAFY. Displays a redundant function with CAULIFLOWER in the up-
CC       regulation of LEAFY. Together with AGL24 and SVP, controls the
CC       identity of the floral meristem and regulates expression of class
CC       B, C and E genes. Represses flowering time genes AGL24, SVP and
CC       SOC1 in emerging floral meristems. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11283333,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17428825, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17794879,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19656343, ECO:0000269|Ref.8}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer capable of binding to CArG-box sequences.
CC       Heterodimer with SEP3, AP1 and SVP. Binds AP3/PI to form a ternary
CC       complex. Interacts with the SEU-LUG corepressor complex when
CC       complexed to AGL24 or SVP. Interacts with AGL15 and AGL16
CC       (PubMed:15805477, PubMed:16679456). Interacts with TT16/AGL32
CC       (PubMed:16080001). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15805477,
