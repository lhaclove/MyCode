CC   -!- FUNCTION: Probable transcription factor that promotes early floral
CC       meristem identity in synergy with APETALA1 and CAULIFLOWER. Is
CC       required subsequently for the transition of an inflorescence
CC       meristem into a floral meristem (PubMed:28586421). Seems to be
CC       partially redundant to the function of APETALA1 and CAULIFLOWER in
CC       the up-regulation of LEAFY. Is also required for normal pattern of
CC       cell division, expansion and differentiation during morphogenesis
CC       of the silique (PubMed:28586421). Probably not required for fruit
CC       elongation but instead is required to prevent ectopic activity of
CC       IND. Represses SAUR10 expression in stems and inflorescence
CC       branches (PubMed:28586421). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10648231,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:15035986, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28586421,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9502732}.
