CC   -!- FUNCTION: Possesses beta-glucosidase activity toward 4-methyl-
CC       umbelliferyl-beta-D-glucoside in vitro. Possesses myrosinase
CC       activity toward indol-3-yl-methylglucosinolate (I3M) and 4-
CC       methoxy-indol-3-yl-methylglucosinolate (4MO-I3M) in vivo
CC       (PubMed:19095900). Component of an inducible preinvasion
CC       resistance mechanism that prevents penetration of the nonhost
CC       fungal species B.graminis and E.pisi (PubMed:16293760). Involved
CC       in indole glucosinolate (IGS) activation during pattern-triggered
CC       immunity (PTI). Functions as myrosinase for the breakdown of
CC       flg22-triggered IGS. Required for both callose deposition and
CC       glucosinolate activation during pathogen-triggered resistance
CC       (PubMed:19095898). During fungal attack, required for IGS
CC       activation that mediates broad-spectrum antifungal defense
CC       (PubMed:19095900). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16293760,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:19095898, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19095900}.
