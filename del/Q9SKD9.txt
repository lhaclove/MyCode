CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transcription factor involved in the regulation of
CC       osmotic stress responses and stomatal movement (PubMed:24773321).
CC       Interacts specifically with the W box (5'-(T)TGAC[CT]-3'), a
CC       frequently occurring elicitor-responsive cis-acting element (By
CC       similarity). Positive regulator of EDS1-dependent defense against
CC       E.amylovora (PubMed:22316300). Together with WRKY70 and WRKY53,
CC       promotes resistance to P.syringae, probably by enhancing salicylic
CC       acid (SA)- dependent genes. Contributes to the suppression of
CC       jasmonic acid (MeJA)-induced expression of PDF1.2
CC       (PubMed:22325892). Together with WRKY54 and WRKY70, promotes
CC       brassinosteroid (BR)-regulated plant growth but prevent drought
CC       response by modulating gene expression (PubMed:28576847).
CC       {ECO:0000250, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22316300,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22325892, ECO:0000269|PubMed:24773321,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:28576847}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Binds to BZR2/BES1 to cooperatively regulate the
CC       expression of target genes. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28576847}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Nucleus {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-
CC       ProRule:PRU00223}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in guard cells, hypocotyls, and in
CC       the vascular tissues of cotyledon and root (PubMed:24773321).
CC       Mostly expressed in roots, at lower levels in leaves and petioles,
CC       and, to a lower extent, in stems, flowers and siliques
CC       (PubMed:22325892). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22325892,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24773321}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Up-regulated by drought, salt and hydrogen peroxide
CC       treatments (PubMed:24773321). Induced by salicylic acid (SA)
CC       (PubMed:22268143, PubMed:22325892). Up-regulated by E.amylovora
CC       (PubMed:22316300). Triggered by P.syringae (PubMed:22325892).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22268143, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22316300,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22325892, ECO:0000269|PubMed:24773321}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylated and destabilized by ASK7/BIN2.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:28576847}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Decreased tolerance to dehydration and salt
CC       stress (PubMed:24773321). Increase in the number of necrotic
CC       leaves and in the intensity of necrosis in response to E.amylovora
CC       (PubMed:22316300). Increased susceptibility to P.syringae
CC       associated with reduced PR1 induction in double mutants wrky46
CC       wrky70 and wrky46 wrky53,and triple mutant wrky46 wrky70 wrky53.
CC       In these mutants, higher induction of PDF1.2 upon jasmonic acid
CC       (MeJA) treatment (PubMed:22325892). The triple mutant wrky46
CC       wrky54 wrky70 has defects in brassinosteroid (BR)-regulated growth
CC       and is more tolerant to drought stress (PubMed:28576847).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22316300, ECO:0000269|PubMed:22325892,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:24773321, ECO:0000269|PubMed:28576847}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the WRKY group III family. {ECO:0000305}.
